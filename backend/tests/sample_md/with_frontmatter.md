# Diabetes Mellitus

Diabetes mellitus is a group of metabolic disorders characterized by
chronic hyperglycemia. **Type 1 diabetes** results from autoimmune
destruction of pancreatic beta cells. **Type 2 diabetes** is caused by
insulin resistance combined with inadequate insulin secretion.

## Treatment

Metformin is first-line therapy for type 2 diabetes. Insulin is required
for all patients with type 1 diabetes and for many with advanced type 2
disease.
